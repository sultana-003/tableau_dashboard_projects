## Sales Performance Analysis Dashboard (Tableau)
### Project Overview
This project presents an **interactive Tableau dashboard** built using the **Superstore sales dataset** to analyze overall sales performance, profitability, product trends, and regional insights.

The dashboard helps stakeholders quickly understand **how sales are performing over time**, **which products and regions drive revenue**, and **where business improvements are needed**.
### Dataset Information
- **Dataset Name:** Superstore Dataset
- **Source:** Kaggle
- **Format:** CSV
- **Key Fields:**
  - Order Date
  - Sales
  - Profit
  - Category
  - Sub-Category
  - Region
  - State
  - Segment
  - Customer Name
### Objectives
- Analyze yearly and monthly sales trends
- Identify top-performing and loss-making products
- Evaluate regional and segment-wise sales performance
- Track key business KPIs using interactive visuals
- Enable dynamic year-based analysis using parameters

### 1️⃣ Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Total Sales  
- Total Profit  
- Profit Margin (%) 

### 2️⃣ Sales Trend Analysis
- Yearly sales trend
- Monthly sales pattern
- Parameter-based year selector for dynamic analysis

### 3️⃣ Product Performance
- Sales by Category
- Profit by Sub-Category (highlighting negative profit items)

### 4️⃣ Regional Performance
- Sales by Region
- State-wise sales performance using map visualization

### 5️⃣ Customer & Segment Insights
- Sales by Customer Segment
- Top 10 Customers by Sales
- Calculated Fields
- Filters & Interactive Controls
- Bar Charts, Line Charts, Pie Charts
- Geographic Maps
- Dashboard & Story Points

###  Key Insights
- Technology category generates the highest sales
- Some sub-categories show consistent negative profit
- West region outperforms other regions
- Sales peak during Q4 (November–December)
- A small group of customers contributes a large share of revenue

### Business Recommendations
- Focus marketing efforts on high-profit categories
- Review pricing and costs for loss-making sub-categories
- Strengthen presence in high-performing regions
- Plan inventory and promotions around Q4 demand spikes
- Introduce loyalty programs for high-value customers
### Tools Used
- Tableau Desktop / Tableau Public
- CSV Data Source (Superstore Dataset)
